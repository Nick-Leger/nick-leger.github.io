Capstone · EML4501 · Fall 2025

Talon Tether — CubeSat Tether Deployment System

Compact tether deployment & charge-management system for two 12U SmallSats. Fits in 3U+1U CubeSat volume; deploys 50–150 m of Aluminum 5056-H18 conductive wire to generate artificial lunar/Martian gravity (0.166–0.38 g) in LEO.

SolidWorks CAD FEA / FOS Analysis Materials Selection 3D Prototyping Stepper Motor Control

I led design analysis: tether wire storage capacity, artificial gravity calculations, material selection via Ansys Granta, motor torque FOS (2.48), tether tension analysis (FOS 7.59), and level wind thread pitch. Our prototype confirmed structural soundness of the dual-satellite reel + level-wind system.

3D-printed prototype · FDM custom parts + OTS hardware · Aluminum 5056-H18 tether wire
