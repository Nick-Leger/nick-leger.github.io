Capstone · EML4501 · Fall 2025

Talon Tether — CubeSat Tether Deployment System

Compact tether deployment and charge-management system for two 12U SmallSats. Fits within a 3U+1U CubeSat volume, deploys 50–150m of Aluminum 5056-H18 conductive wire, and generates lunar (0.166g) or Martian (0.38g) artificial gravity in LEO.

SolidWorks CADStructural FOS Analysis Ansys Material SelectionDFM 3D PrototypingMotor Control

My Role: Design lead for the reel feed mechanism, reel locking mechanism, and overall assembly structure. Responsible for all design analysis: tether wire storage capacity calculations, artificial gravity angular velocity derivation, tether material selection via Ansys Granta (Aluminum 5056-H18), motor torque FOS analysis (realized FOS 2.48), tether tension analysis (realized FOS 7.59), level wind thread pitch calculation, frame rod analysis (FOS 12.0), and bolt/joint analysis. Also led design for manufacturing process and prototyping.

3D-printed FDM prototype with OTS hardware. The reel subsystem (stepper motor + gearbox, spool, level wind) fits within a 3U CubeSat frame. The 1U daughter satellite carries the tether attachment clamp and inline load cell for closed-loop tension monitoring.

📄 Full report available: Download Capstone Report (PDF)

Coursework · FEA · 2026

Bike Frame FEA — Abaqus Optimization

Optimized the diameter of 8 bike frame tube segments using Fully Stressed Design (FSD) and buckling analysis in Abaqus to minimize total weight while supporting given loads and preventing buckling under all load cases.

Abaqus FEAFully Stressed Design Buckling AnalysisStructural OptimizationMATLAB

Used iterative FSD methodology to resize each of the 8 tube cross-sections so every critical member reaches its allowable stress simultaneously — the theoretical minimum-weight solution. Verified against first-mode buckling eigenvalues to ensure stability margins were maintained.

📄 Download Full FEA Report (PDF) — includes FSD iteration tables, final tube diameters, buckling eigenvalue progression, and bending moment diagrams.
